## Venture Description

FreakingGenius (FG) is an AI-powered math tutoring service for middle school students who are underperforming in math.

Product: Dual-experience offering combining (1) an AI chatbot that replaces the role of a human tutor, and (2) a tablet application that analyzes the student's work in real-time and feeds observations back to the chatbot. Interaction between the chatbot and the student is voice-based.

Differentiation: Real-time handwriting analysis, voice-based tutoring, and personalized content generation.

Business model: $79/month subscription. Hardware delivered via BNPL partner at $25–$35/month over 36 months; student owns the tablet. Tablet is branded with FG stickers (semi-white-label feel).

Target customer: Middle school students struggling in math, with parents as the paying decision-maker.
