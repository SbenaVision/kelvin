## Venture Description

ok so the idea is basically — a lot of middle schoolers are struggling in math and parents don't know what to do, private tutors are expensive and inconsistent etc. so we want to build something that actually helps them.

it's two things together — there's a chatbot that acts like a tutor, and a tablet app that watches what the kid is writing and tells the chatbot what's going on so the chatbot actually knows what the kid is doing. they talk with voice, not typing. we think the combination is pretty unique, no one is doing both.

we'd do personalized content too, I think that's where it gets really powerful.

pricing — $79/month for the service. the tablet we'd give through a BNPL partner, kid pays like $25-35/month for 36 months and then owns it. we'd put FG stickers on it so it feels like our thing even though it's not really our hardware.

called FG (FreakingGenius).
