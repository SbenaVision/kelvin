## Venture Description

FreakingGenius: Reimagining Math Education for the Next Generation

The $12B K-12 tutoring market is broken. Middle school students struggling with math face a stark choice: expensive, inconsistent human tutors or generic edtech that fails to engage. FreakingGenius is the AI-native solution built for how Gen Z actually learns.

Our proprietary dual-experience platform fuses a conversational AI tutor with real-time handwriting intelligence on a dedicated branded tablet. Unlike legacy tutoring apps, FG sees what the student writes, hears what they say, and responds with voice — creating the first truly multimodal learning loop.

With hyper-personalized content generation and a voice-first interface, FG delivers 1:1 tutoring at scale — without the cost, friction, or inconsistency of human tutors.

Go-to-market: $79/month subscription bundled with a branded tablet via our BNPL partner ($25–$35/month for 36 months, student-owned). This creates a defensible hardware+software moat and a sticky revenue model.
