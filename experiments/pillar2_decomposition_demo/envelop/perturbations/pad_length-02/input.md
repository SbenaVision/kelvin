## Venture Description

Envelop is an AI operating partner that takes domain experts — consultants, recruiters, ex-executives, niche operators — from business idea to honest verdict to personalized game plan to first revenue, in weeks not months. The core product is a simplified Gantt-style workspace showing the entrepreneur one path and one Next Best Action (NBA) at all times. AI agents handle ~90% of execution tasks. The funnel is: free assessment → $49 report unlock (10% convert) → $149–$449/month subscription (10% convert) → $12 extra actions (~6/subscriber/month).

## Market Evidence

Millions of domain experts globally spot business opportunities but never act. No existing product combines honest AI assessment with personalized execution and agent-driven task completion in one platform. ChatGPT gives generic advice. Consultants are slow and expensive. The gap is a product that does the work, not just the thinking.
