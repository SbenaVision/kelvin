## Target Customer

Vertical-SaaS platforms with 5,000+ end-customer businesses in operational-tech, property-management, healthcare-back-office, or restaurant-operations verticals, where the platform already moves money on behalf of customers and is looking to layer in treasury and card issuance.

## Unit Economics

Blended take rate 1.8% of card-spend GMV plus a $0.40 per-transaction fee. Average customer GMV $14M annualized. Customer gross margin 67% after sponsor-bank revenue share and network interchange splits. Sales cycle 5.5 months. CAC $340K per logo, LTV $4.1M at assumed 7-year tenure. Burn $1.4M/month on $62M cash remaining.

## Market Evidence

The embedded-finance category is a $7B+ revenue market growing 30%+ annually per industry analysts. Rhodium's sponsor bank relationship was signed 14 months ago and the BIN is live. Card issuance through similar platforms has reached $30B+ in annualized volume in the last two years. Category leadership is contested, with three funded competitors pursuing adjacent segments.

## Gate Rule

Advance to Scale requires: durable recurring revenue at or above $10M ARR, a proven distribution motion that does not depend on founder heroics, regulatory standing sufficient to support the next wave of sponsor-bank or direct-issuer relationships, and a reproducible path from mid-stage prospect to live customer. All conditions are met. Annual revenue run-rate has crossed $10M. Outbound motion is producing qualified pipeline without founder direct involvement. Last quarter's regulatory exam closed with no material findings. 22 customers are in production and the median time-from-signed-contract to live-card-issuance is under 60 days.

22 platform customers in production. $860K monthly recurring revenue, up from $340K twelve months ago. Annual revenue run-rate $10.3M. Series A closed eight months ago at a $90M post-money valuation; Series B process initiated with three term sheets received. Regulatory exam passed with no material findings last quarter.

## Venture Description

Rhodium is a fintech issuing embedded-finance debit cards and treasury accounts for vertical-SaaS platforms that want to offer a bank-like experience to their own customers without becoming a bank. Rhodium handles the BIN sponsorship, KYC orchestration, settlement, and compliance reporting; the platform customer brands and controls the UX. Go-to-market is direct to the platform's finance or product leadership.
