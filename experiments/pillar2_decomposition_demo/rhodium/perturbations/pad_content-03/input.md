## Venture Description

Rhodium is a fintech issuing embedded-finance debit cards and treasury accounts for vertical-SaaS platforms that want to offer a bank-like experience to their own customers without becoming a bank. Rhodium handles the BIN sponsorship, KYC orchestration, settlement, and compliance reporting; the platform customer brands and controls the UX. Go-to-market is direct to the platform's finance or product leadership.

## Target Customer

Vertical-SaaS platforms with 5,000+ end-customer businesses in operational-tech, property-management, healthcare-back-office, or restaurant-operations verticals, where the platform already moves money on behalf of customers and is looking to layer in treasury and card issuance.

## Venture Description

Envelop is an AI operating partner that takes domain experts — consultants, recruiters, ex-executives, niche operators — from business idea to honest verdict to personalized game plan to first revenue, in weeks not months. The core product is a simplified Gantt-style workspace showing the entrepreneur one path and one Next Best Action (NBA) at all times. AI agents handle ~90% of execution tasks. The funnel is: free assessment → $49 report unlock (10% convert) → $149–$449/month subscription (10% convert) → $12 extra actions (~6/subscriber/month).

## Team

Four co-founders: ex-Head of Risk from a Tier-1 issuing platform, ex-CTO of a fintech that exited to a top-five card network, compliance officer with 18 years in bank examinations, and an enterprise sales leader who built out the embedded-finance channel at a competitor. Team of 27 across engineering, compliance, and go-to-market. Headquartered in New York with a compliance office in Washington DC.
